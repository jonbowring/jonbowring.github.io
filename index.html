How it works

  1. Fill in my commission form (linked below and on my instagram profile @bl1ssdom)!
  2. Describe what you'd like, whether its half body, has a background, any extra characters etc.
  3. Send inspiration/reference pictures (pinterest boards are especially helpful!) and/or a description
  4. Pay 50% upfront via PayPal
  5. I'll send progress pics so you can let me know what you like and what you want changed :)
  6. I'll send the finished product, you can pay the remaining 50% and then enjoy your art!<3
